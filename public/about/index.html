About

I am a fourth-year mathematics major with a concentration in statistics at Grinnell College.

My undergraduate studies span probability theory, data science, and statistical/machine learning, and I code in Python and R.

I work as a peer mentor at Grinnell College’s Data Analysis and Social Inquiry Lab, a grader for the statistics department, and a member of the Statistics SEPC – a student government body that assists in the faculty hiring and review process within their respective departments, among other responsibilities.

At the moment, I am applying to graduate programs in Biostatistics. Feel free to contact me if you would like to discuss academia or about the field of biostatistics/heath data science!
